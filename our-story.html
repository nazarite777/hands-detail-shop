The Journey

🏠

1990s — Baton Rouge Roots

My story begins in Baton Rouge as the eldest of five children. My uncle showed me something profound: a perfectly detailed vehicle isn't just clean — it transforms how people feel about themselves. That moment planted a seed that would grow for decades.

🌊

2005 — Hurricane Katrina's Impact

At 16, Hurricane Katrina changed everything. Life took an unexpected turn as I moved north from Louisiana. Instead of letting circumstances define me, I embraced the challenge and moved to Ohio to start fresh. Adversity became my greatest teacher.

✈️

2007–2011 — Air Force Service

After high school in New Concord, Ohio, I answered the call to serve my country in the U.S. Air Force. Working on diesel and jet engines taught me that perfection truly IS in the details. Military precision became second nature — something I carry into every detail job today.

🏆

2010–Present — 16 Years of Excellence

For 16 years, I've been turning that passion into reality. From daily drivers to yachts, RVs to aircraft — every vehicle receives the same military-grade attention to detail. I've detailed over 5,000 vehicles and built a reputation for excellence that speaks for itself. This isn't just what I do; it's who I am.
