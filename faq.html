General Questions

What is auto detailing?

+

Auto detailing is a comprehensive cleaning, restoration, and finishing process that goes far beyond a regular car wash. We meticulously clean both the interior and exterior of your vehicle, addressing every surface with specialized products and techniques to restore it to like-new condition.

Our detailing services include paint correction, ceramic coating, deep interior cleaning, leather conditioning, engine bay cleaning, and much more depending on the package selected.

How long does a detail typically take?

+

The duration varies depending on the package and your vehicle's condition:

  • Essential Package: 2-3 hours
  • Executive Package: 4-5 hours
  • Signature Package: 6-8 hours
  • Presidential Package: 1-2 days
  • Ultimate Armor Package: 2-3 days

These are estimates. Heavily soiled vehicles or those requiring extensive paint correction may take longer.

What is ceramic coating and do I need it?

+

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your vehicle's paint, creating a protective layer that lasts 2-5+ years (depending on the quality).

Benefits:

  • Extreme hydrophobic properties (water beads and slides off)
  • Protection against UV damage, oxidation, and chemical stains
  • Easier to clean and maintain
  • Enhanced gloss and depth of color
  • Scratch resistance (not scratch-proof, but more resistant)

If you want long-term protection and plan to keep your vehicle for years, ceramic coating is an excellent investment.

Can you remove scratches and swirl marks?

+

Yes! Paint correction is our specialty. We can remove or significantly reduce:

  • Swirl marks and holograms
  • Light to moderate scratches
  • Water spots and etching
  • Oxidation and fading
  • Buffer trails from improper detailing

Our Presidential and Ultimate Armor packages include multi-stage paint correction. Deep scratches that penetrate through the clear coat may not be fully removable without repainting.

What do I need to provide for a mobile detail?

+

We bring everything we need! All we require is:

  • Access to electricity: Standard 110V outlet within 50 feet
  • Water access: Outdoor spigot/hose connection (or we can bring our own water tank for an additional fee)
  • Parking space: Driveway, parking lot, or similar flat surface

For locations without water access, let us know in advance and we'll bring our own supply.

Do I need to be present during the service?

+

No, you don't need to be present the entire time. Many customers:

  • Go about their day at home while we work
  • Continue working in their home office
  • Leave for errands and return when we're done

We just need someone available at the start to hand over keys and at the end for payment and walkthrough. We'll text/call when we're finished.

What if it rains on the day of my appointment?

+

We monitor weather forecasts closely. If rain is expected:

  • We'll contact you 24 hours in advance to discuss rescheduling
  • Light rain? Interior-only services can still be performed
  • We can work in covered areas (garage, carport) when available
  • No fees for weather-related rescheduling

Your satisfaction and the quality of our work come first, so we'll never rush a detail due to weather.
